Deaf Reach Schools and Training Centers, a program of Family Educational Services Foundation (FESF)

Deaf Reach Schools, Colleges & Training Centers

Deaf Reach Schools and Training Centers, a program of Family Educational Services Foundation (FESF), envisions a future where education is a reality for all deaf children in Pakistan. By investing in PSL learning resources, Deaf Reach is working to make literacy, education and skills training accessible to deaf students, and to capacitate teachers and families.
